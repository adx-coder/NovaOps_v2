### Scene 1.1: The Silent Server Room
**Prompt:** Cinematic wide shot of a dark, high-tech server room at night. Blue and white LED light flashes occasionally from server racks. Shallow depth of field. 4k resolution, hyper-realistic, low lighting.
**Motion:** Slow forward dolly zoom into the darkness of the server aisle.

### Scene 1.2: The Pager Alert
**Prompt:** Extreme close-up of a modern smartphone on a dark wooden nightstand. The screen glows brightly in the dark room with a red notification block that says "CRITICAL INCIDENT: OOM EXCEPTION". Glitchy digital textures. 
**Motion:** The phone vibrates slightly, causing a ripple in a nearby glass of water.

### Scene 2.1: Concept: The Digital War Room
**Prompt:** Abstract 3D visualization of a "Digital War Room". Translucent blue architectural lines assembling into a circular table in a void. Data streams like rain in the background. Matrix-style aesthetics but polished and modern.
**Motion:** The circular table "constructs" itself from digital voxels as the camera rotates around it.

### Scene 3.1: The Specialist Agents
**Prompt:** Close up on four glowing digital icons or avatars hovering in a row. One represents a folder (logs), one a gear (infra), one a graph (metrics), and one a magnifying glass (investigation). Elegant, minimalist design. 
**Motion:** The icons pulse with light sequentially as if communicating.

### Scene 4.1: The Blind Jury
**Prompt:** A cinematic shot of a dark courtroom bench where three glowing, faceless digital silhouettes (the Jurors) sit in judgement. Volumetric light rays (God rays) coming from above. High contrast, epic scale.
**Motion:** The middle juror nods slowly.

### Scene 5.1: The Voice of Reason
**Prompt:** Cinematic shot of a hand picking up a glowing smartphone. The screen displays a waveform that reacts to a voice. "NOVA SONIC" text is visible. Cinematic bokeh in the background.
**Motion:** The waveform moves dynamically, casting light on the person's face.
